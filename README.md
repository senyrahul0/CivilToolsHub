# CivilToolsHub

## 🚀 India's Construction Engineering & Safety Platform

CivilToolsHub is an SEO-first engineering platform designed to help:

- Civil Engineers
- Site Engineers
- Safety Officers
- Contractors
- Builders
- Quantity Surveyors
- Engineering Students

---

## 🎯 Mission

Build India's most trusted construction engineering platform by providing free professional engineering tools, calculators, AI-powered safety utilities, technical resources, and educational content.
